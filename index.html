Darrell Seale

Scuba Instructor · Therapeutic Diving Advocate · Veteran

PADI and SDI-certified scuba instructor with over 2,500 dives and more than 300 certified students. Based in Abu Dhabi, UAE, Darrell Seale has built a career around underwater exploration, diver education, and using scuba diving as a tool for healing and personal transformation.

About Darrell

A Life Built Around Diving

Darrell Seale is a scuba diving instructor and co-founder of Patriot Divers, a nonprofit organization that uses scuba diving as a form of therapy for wounded, injured, and disabled veterans. His work focuses on helping people build confidence, overcome physical and psychological barriers, and reconnect through shared underwater experiences.

Through decades of diving across diverse environments, Darrell has developed a teaching approach centered on safety, discipline, and confidence-building. He has certified hundreds of divers and continues to work with students in the UAE, where he actively participates in the local diving community.

His commitment to diving goes beyond instruction. As part of Patriot Divers, he has helped design programs that integrate scuba diving into rehabilitation and recovery, demonstrating how structured underwater activity can support mental clarity, resilience, and teamwork.

Service, Leadership, and Global Experience

Before focusing on diving full-time, Darrell served as a U.S. Air Force officer and later held senior roles in the aerospace and defense industry. This background shaped his structured, safety-first approach to diving instruction and program development.

He has also participated in community initiatives including the Special Olympics World Games Abu Dhabi 2019 and continues to engage in veteran-focused nonprofit work.

Continuous Exploration

Darrell brings a global perspective shaped by extensive international travel and decades of cross-cultural experience. Today, that perspective is applied directly to diving—training students, supporting veterans, and promoting scuba as a pathway to resilience, discipline, and discovery.
